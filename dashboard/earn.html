How does URL Shorteners work?

URL Shorteners help you to shorten your URLs, at the same time it helps you to earn some income through ad revenue.

  1. Shorten the URLs of your blog that is created from our website
  2. URL shorteners provide you the shortened link
  3. Share the shortened link through your social media
  4. Whenever the user clicks on the link, they will be redirected to a page where they view the ad servred by the URL shortners for 30 seconds
  5. After 30 seconds the user will be redirected to your blog page
  6. Thereby you will earn commission based on the number of people who clicks & views the ad for 30 second
